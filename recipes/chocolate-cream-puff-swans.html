Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Stir water, butter, sugar and salt together
  3. Stir eggs into dough
  4. Pipe 8 tapered ovals of dough
  5. Bake in preheated oven
  6. Pipe out S-shapes of dough
