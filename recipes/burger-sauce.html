Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayo
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 2 tsp dried minced onion
  • 1/4 tsp dried minced garlic
  • 1/8 tsp white vinegar
  • hot sauce
  • seasoned pepper

Directions

  1. Whisk together mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic, and vinegar in a bowl.
  2. Season with hot sauce and seasoned pepper.
  3. Cover and chill at least 1 hour before serving.
